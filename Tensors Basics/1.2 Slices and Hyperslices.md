## 1 - Tensors and Their Subparts

### 1.2 - Slices and Hyperslices

>**Definition (Slices of 3-way Tensor)** <br>
> Let $X$ be a 3-way tensor of size $m \times n \times p$. The $i_{th}$ **horizontal slice** is a matrix of size $n \times p$ given by $\textbf{X}(i,:,:)$. The $j_{th}$ **lateral slice** is a matrix of size $m \times p$ given by $\textbf{X}(:,j,:)$. The $k_{th}$ **frontal slice** is a matrix of size $m \times n$ given by $\textbf{X}(:,:,k)$.
